**期望**
$$
E(X)=\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}f(x)xdx=\sum p(x_i)x_i
$$

- $$E(X+c)=E(X)+c,E(cX)=cE(X)$$
- $$E(X^2)=\sum p x^2 $$
**方差**

衡量偏离期望的程度.
$$
E((X-E(X))^2)=E(X^2+E^2-2EX)=E(X^2-2EX)+E^2
\\=\sum p(x^2-2Ex)+E^2=E(X^2)-2E^2+E^2\\=E(X^2)-E^2=D(X)
$$
